Featured Recipes

Italian Pastas

Spaghetti alla carbonara

Spaghetti alla carbonara consists of pasta with cured pork, pecorino romano cheese, eggs, and black pepper.

Spaghetti aglio e olio

'Spaghetti with garlic and [olive] oil' is a pasta dish popular because it is simple to prepare and makes use of inexpensive, readily available ingredients that have long shelf lives in a pantry.

Indian Curries

Lamb Korma

Lamb Khorma is a rich and creamy Indian curry made with tender pieces of lamb simmered in a flavorful sauce, often including yogurt, cashews, and a blend of spices.

Paneer Lababdar

Paneer Lababdar is a rich and creamy paneer gravy dish with onions, tomatoes, cashews and spices.

Mexican Tacos

Carnitas Tacos

'Little meats tacos' are made with slow-cooked, seasoned pork that is shredded and served in warm tortillas. They are typically topped with ingredients like diced onions, cilantro, and lime for added flavor.

Carne Asada Tacos

Carne Asada Tacos are made with grilled, marinated beef, typically flank or skirt steak, served in warm corn tortillas. They are usually topped with diced onions, fresh cilantro, and a squeeze of lime for added flavor.

Japanese Sushi

Umekyu and Cucumber Maki

Umekyu and Cucumber Maki is composed of pickled plum (umeboshi in Japanese) and fresh cucumber, rolled in sushi rice and roasted nori (seaweed). The pickled plum gives off a sweet taste whilst the crunch and juices of the cucumber adds a fresh element to the roll.

Oshinko Maki

'Pickled daikon roll' is a type of salt-pickled white radish (takuan in Japanese) that has a sweet sourness to it. It is wrapped in sushi rice and roasted nori (seaweed).
