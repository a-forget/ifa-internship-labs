Discover the Wild North

Aaron's Awesome Adventures is made up of a dedicated group of passionate people who share a love for adventure, nature, and the North. We believe there's an explorer in everyone that yearns for adventure and we are happy to get to bring that experience to you in the wild and beautiful North!


We offer a variety of guided camping, hiking, trekking, kayaking, canoeing, and star gazing tours across the Yukon, Alaska, British Columbia, and the Canadian Rockies. We operate year-round and have adventures tailored for each skill level and activity, ranging from one to twenty-one days in length.
